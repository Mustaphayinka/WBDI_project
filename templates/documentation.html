1. Introduction

The World Bank Data Download App is designed to provide easy access to World Bank data related to Nigeria. You can explore a wide range of data categories and download selected data for your research and analysis.

2. Getting Started

Accessing the App

To access the app, simply visit https://wbdidata.onrender.com/ from your web browser.

3. Using the App

Selecting Data

On the main page, you'll find a list of available data options related to Nigeria.

Use the search bar to find specific data options by name.

Adding Data to Selection

  1. Select a data option from the list.
  2. Click the "Add to Selected Data" button.
  3. The selected data will appear in the "Selected Data" section.

Downloading Data

  1. Select one or more data options.
  2. Click the "Download Selected Data" button.
  3. The app will prepare your data for download and save the data to your device.

Known Limitations

Please be aware of the following limitations when using this app:

  • Access to data is limited to information related to Nigeria.
  • The app does not provide data for other countries or regions.
